Food Offerings

Octopus Falafel Truck

Welcome to Falafel Truck! Every day is a different experience of Middle Eastern and Mediterranean cuisine. Everything is prepared fresh and cooked on my charcoal grill. Each day is a special selection from the Chef to make sure each bite is fresh and right off the grill. Every dish has been specially prepared to match flavors and create an eating experience that is sure to bring a smile, or at least that is my wish to everyone who comes by.

Dos Hermanos Tacos

Two brothers, a passion for cooking, and a love for their culture is what Dos Hermanos Tacos is all about. We are a Philadelphia based food truck, started back in November of 2014, using fresh ingredients and authentic spices to create food typical of our heritage in Mexico. Now we are a destination spot for authentic Mexican food and a premier caterer for a variety of events.

Chewys Food Truck

Founded by Charlie Sokowski, Chewys Food Truck has been proudly serving up some of Philly's most beloved comfort foods since 2011. Charlie pulls inspiration from is family's cooking, kitchens from his past, and his travels around the country. Specializing in smoked meats, fried chicken, burgers and breakfast items. Chewy's loves to bring the crowd, feed and entertain parties of any size.
